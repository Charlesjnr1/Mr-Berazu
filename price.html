Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our pricing and services

How do I know which pricing model is right for my business?

During our free consultation, we'll assess your business needs, goals, and budget to recommend the most suitable engagement model. We consider factors like project scope, timeline, and required expertise.

Do you offer discounts for long-term engagements?

Yes, we offer discounted rates for extended retainer agreements and large-scale projects. Contact us to discuss custom pricing for your long-term needs.

Can I switch between pricing models?

Absolutely. We understand that business needs evolve. You can transition between our pricing models as your requirements change, with proper notice and agreement on new terms.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and checks. Retainer clients can set up automated monthly payments for convenience.

Do you offer custom packages?

Yes, we understand that every business has unique needs. We can create custom packages that combine elements from different pricing models to best suit your requirements.
